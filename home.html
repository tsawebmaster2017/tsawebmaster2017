About IHSTSA

Ithaca High School TSA is a chapter located in central New York.

Technology Teachers

The technology department of Ithaca High School provides tons of classes encompassing all aspects of technology. The teachers aspire to prepare students for future learning and employment.
Scott "Scooter" Briegle
Not only is he an advisor for our TSA chapter, Mr. Briegle is the head of the technology department at Ithaca High School. Teaching courses like Computer Integrated Manufacturing and Engineering Design and Development, he is a great proponent of STEM at Ithaca High School.
Contact him at: sbriegle@icsd.k12.ny.us
Ian Krywe
The head of Code Red Robotics and popular among students for his easy going personality, Mr. Krywe teaches Design and Drawing Production.
Contact him at: ikrywe@icsd.k12.ny.us
William Sauve
Renowned for his love of cars, Mr. Sauve teaches Design and Drawing Production as well as several other classes including Consumer Automotive.
Contact him at: wsauve@icsd.k12.ny.us
Roger Sevilla
Mr. Sevilla teaches many classes, including Architectural Design and Drivers Ed. Always there for his students, Mr. Sevilla's classes are very popular.
Contact him at: rsevilla@icsd.k12.ny.us
Jonathan Shyne
Creative, and head of TV Production Club, Mr. Shyne teaches Digital Media Production. Available to give advice outside of class, many students look to Mr. Shyne for answers when they need help editing a video for projects unrelated to his classes.
Contact him at: jonathan.shyne@icsd.k12.ny.us
